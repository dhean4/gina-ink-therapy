II About the studio

A soft place to receive
something permanent.

Gina Ink Therapy is a private tattoo studio tucked into a quiet corner of Abule-Egba, Lagos. It began with a simple belief — that getting a tattoo should feel as considered as the design itself.

The work leans quiet and intentional: fine lines, botanical forms, delicate script, and personal symbols drawn one at a time. No flash walls, no pressure — just careful ink, clean tools, and long, good conversations.

— Gina Founder & Lead Artist

IVThe Process

Four quiet
steps, start
to finish.

From the first DM to your aftercare routine — here's exactly how a session at the Abule-Egba studio unfolds.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    A relaxed DM or call. Share your reference, the feeling you want, the placement. I'll tell you honestly if I'm the right artist for it.

    15 min · Free
  2. 02

    Custom Design

    I draw your piece from scratch — usually 2–3 drafts, refined together until it feels unmistakably yours. A small deposit holds your date.

    1–2 days · Two revisions included
  3. 03

    The Session

    Private, unhurried, and built around you. Tea, music of your choosing, breaks whenever you need them. Clean tools, single-use needles — always.

    1–5 hours · Abule-Egba studio
  4. 04

    Aftercare

    You leave with a printed aftercare ritual, a small kit, and my number. I check in on day three and week two. If anything feels off, you reach me directly.

    Within 1 week · Touch-ups free in 90 days
